Privacy Policy

P.A.N.T. Rapid Sort

Last Updated: April 2026

Introduction

Welcome to P.A.N.T. Rapid Sort ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application (the "Application").

This privacy policy applies to P.A.N.T. Rapid Sort available on Google Play Store and other app platforms. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account & Identity Information

  • Google Account Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we collect your Google account information including display name, email address, and profile photo URL.
  • Profile Information: Username, profile photo, and any other information you choose to provide.
  • Firebase User ID: A unique identifier generated by Firebase for authentication and data synchronization.

1.2 Gameplay & Progress Data

  • Game Progress: Level, XP points, coins, high scores, and game streaks.
  • Collected Words: Words you've sorted and collected in your Sticker Books.
  • Claimed Rewards: Information about rewards claimed and collected.
  • Inventory & Power-ups: Card quantities, loadout selections, and power-up usage.
  • Customization Choices: Theme selections and other personalization preferences.
  • Daily Chest State: Information about daily rewards and chest progression.

1.3 Leaderboard & Social Data

  • Leaderboard Display Name: Your chosen display name on the leaderboard.
  • Leaderboard Level & Score: Your level and score are shared on the global leaderboard for competitive ranking.
  • Streak Points: Your best streak achievements displayed publicly.

1.4 Support & Device Information

  • Support Tickets: Subject, description, issue type, player ID, app version, device model, and operating system.
  • Device & Operational Signals: Connectivity status, device information, and local storage (SharedPreferences).
  • Diagnostic Data: Crash reports, error logs, and app performance metrics.

1.5 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secures user sign-in and account authentication.
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores and syncs your game data, progress, leaderboard data, and support tickets across devices.
  • Google Sign-In: Enables Google account authentication.
  • Google Mobile Ads / AdMob: Serves ads and collects ad-related identifiers and usage signals (varies based on ad configuration).
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized data about user behavior, feature usage, and app performance.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Automatically collects crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Application and its features
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your account
  • To send you updates, newsletters, and promotional content (with your consent)
  • To respond to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • To monitor app performance, fix bugs, and prevent fraudulent activities
  • To analyze user behavior and improve user experience
  • To customize content and features based on your preferences
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Log Data & Crash Reports

When you use our Application, our servers automatically log information including:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages accessed and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or app
  • App version and build information
  • Operating system details
  • Crash reports and error logs

This Log Data is used to diagnose technical problems, maintain security, track trends, and improve the Application. Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects and reports crash data to help us identify and fix stability issues.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Application and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. You can request deletion of your account at any time by following the process outlined on our Account Deletion page. Certain data may be retained for legal, accounting, or legitimate business purposes.

6. Account Deletion

To request deletion of your P.A.N.T. Rapid Sort account and all associated personal data, please visit our Account Deletion page or email us directly. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Please note that some data may be anonymized or aggregated and retained for analytical purposes, and certain data may be retained to comply with legal obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Application is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete such information and terminate the child's account. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Links & Services

The Application may contain links to third-party websites, applications, and services that are not operated by us. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through our Application. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services before providing your information.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications and analytics tracking
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. Your continued use of the Application after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
