Delete Your Account

We understand you may want to delete your account and data from P.A.N.T. Rapid Sort. We're sad to see you go, but we respect your privacy choices. Follow the instructions below to request account deletion.

How to Delete Your Account

📧 Email Support

To request the deletion of your P.A.N.T. Rapid Sort account and all associated data, please send an email to our support team with the following information:

To: jgerongco14@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Email Body:
Please include your username or the email address associated with your account. The more information you provide, the faster we can process your request.

Processing Time: We will process your deletion request within 7 business days of receiving your email. Once your request is processed, your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

What Gets Deleted

⚠️ Important Information

  • • Irreversible Action: Account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, you cannot recover your account, progress, or achievements.
  • • In-App Purchases: Deleted accounts cannot recover in-app purchases or premium features. Refunds are subject to app store policies.
  • • Processing Time: Account deletion may take up to 7 business days to complete across all our systems.
  • • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been successfully deleted.
  • • Contact Verification: We may verify your identity before processing the deletion to protect your account security.

Alternative Options

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

🔐 Pause Your Account

You can temporarily pause your account instead of deleting it. This keeps your data safe while you take a break from the game.

🔔 Manage Notifications

Adjust notification settings in your account preferences to reduce game notifications without deleting your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?

A: No, account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.

Q: How long does deletion take?

A: Account deletion is processed within 7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email when the process is complete.

Q: Will I get a refund for in-app purchases?

A: Refunds for in-app purchases are subject to your app store's policy (Google Play or Apple App Store). Contact them directly for details.

Q: What if I don't provide detailed information?

A: We'll do our best to locate your account with the information provided. Providing a username or email makes the process faster and more accurate.

Q: Will my username be reusable after deletion?

A: Usernames may not be immediately available for reuse. We retain some records for legal and operational purposes.
